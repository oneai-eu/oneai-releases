OneGlue v0.166.0 fixes two long-standing friction points and adds a convenience feature. Remote desktop and VNC sessions now respond to clicks correctly — menus and application buttons are reliably clickable again. Shared OneGlue links now survive login: open a link from a colleague, log in, and land on the exact item rather than the dashboard. To make sharing easier, every item detail page now has a "Copy link" button that puts the right canonical URL on your clipboard in one click.
