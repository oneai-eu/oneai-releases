# OneGlue v0.188.0 — What's New

**Release Date:** 2026-07-09

AI device-analysis assistant, a cleaner device page, favorites, smoother connections, and SCVMM / Hyper-V inventory.

## AI device-analysis assistant

Ask the assistant to analyze a Linux or Windows device for problems — it now works through multiple checks step-by-step and hands you a clear findings report with remediation suggestions. Read-only diagnostics run automatically; anything that could change the system always asks you to confirm first. It replies in your language, you can save any analysis straight to a document, and pressing Enter sends your prompt.

## Cleaner device page

The device detail page is reorganized: the agent, live compute and GPU telemetry, and automation now sit together in one focused "Compute & Automation" panel, and the sidebar is decluttered. Several layout glitches are fixed (copy-link button placement, field spacing).

## Favorites

Star the configurations, networks, credentials and other items you use most — they sort to the top of each list.

## Smoother remote connections

A refreshed connect dialog for RDP, VNC and SSH sessions.

## Keyboard-friendly search

Move through search results with the arrow and Tab keys.

## More control for admins

Choose your workspace's default AI model, and give each AI persona a capability scope — Observe, Diagnose or Remediate — so a read-only persona can never propose changes.

## SCVMM / Hyper-V inventory

Connect an SCVMM environment and map its clouds to your organizations to keep virtual-machine inventory in sync.
