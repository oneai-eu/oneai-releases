OneGlue v0.199.7 makes remote RDP and VNC sessions feel native: the clipboard now works in both directions — paste from your own computer into the remote desktop with Ctrl+V (plus a one-step "Send clipboard" fallback for browsers that restrict clipboard access), and copy from the session back to your machine. Session display is smoother too: switching the resolution (e.g. Match window → 1280×720) applies cleanly without breaking mouse clicks, and click accuracy stays correct across every zoom and fit mode.
