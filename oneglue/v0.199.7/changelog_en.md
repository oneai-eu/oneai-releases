# OneGlue v0.199.7 — What's New

**Release Date:** 2026-07-12

Remote sessions that feel native — copy, paste, and resize just work.

## Copy & paste across your remote sessions — both directions

Working in an RDP or VNC session no longer means fighting the clipboard:

- **Paste into the session** — copy anything on your own computer and paste it straight into the remote desktop with **Ctrl+V**. For browsers that restrict automatic clipboard access, a **Send clipboard** button gives you a reliable one-step fallback.
- **Copy out of the session** — copy inside the remote desktop and paste it back on your own machine.

No more retyping passwords, commands, or config snippets by hand.

## Smoother, more reliable session display

- **Resolution switching fixed.** Changing the session resolution (e.g. *Match window* → *1280×720*) now applies cleanly and **mouse clicks keep working** — previously a switch could leave clicks landing in the wrong place.
- Mouse-click accuracy stays correct across every zoom and fit mode.
