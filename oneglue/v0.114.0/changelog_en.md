# OneGlue v0.114.0 — Remote access & agents for your devices

**Release Date:** 2026-06-22

Documentation is most useful when it's connected to the real devices behind it. This milestone closes that gap: reach and manage your documented devices directly from OneGlue, with live status that tells you what's actually online — and no extra tooling to juggle for day-to-day access.

## Highlights

- **Open a remote desktop right in your browser.** Connect to a device over RDP or VNC from within OneGlue itself — no separate client to install or launch. Resolution presets, fit-to-window, and a keyboard-shortcut helper for tricky key combinations make working in a remote session feel natural.
- **Install a lightweight OneGlue agent on your machines.** The optional agent keeps your device inventory accurate and up to date automatically — no more documentation that drifts out of sync with reality — and reports live reachability so you can see at a glance what's genuinely online.
- **Simple, guided rollout.** A branded installer, per-organization pre-configured downloads, and a system-tray status indicator make deploying the agent across a whole fleet straightforward rather than a project in itself.
- **Fail-safe by design.** Remote sessions check that a device is actually reachable before they start, so you get a clear "offline" status instead of a connection that hangs. These live signals are also what the AI assistant's device actions build on, once your workspace is paired with oneAI.
