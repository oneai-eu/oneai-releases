# OneGlue v0.194.0 — What's New

**Release Date:** 2026-07-11

Know where every VM runs — and whether its replica is healthy.

## Host, cluster and replication at a glance

The VM Fleet now shows, for every virtual machine, **which host and failover cluster it runs on** — right in the overview, no drill-down needed. Alongside it, a **replication status badge** tells you at a glance whether a VM's Hyper-V Replica is healthy (green), needs attention (amber) or is critical (red).

## Full replication detail on click

Open any VM to see its complete replication picture: the **role** (Primary or Recovery), the **health**, and the current **state**. Because replicated VMs exist in two places, they now appear as two entries — the primary and its recovery copy — each clearly labelled, so you can see your geo-redundancy (for example, a VM primary in one datacenter with its recovery copy in another).
