# OneGlue v0.71.0 — Domains & DNS, live

**Release Date:** 2026-05-30

Domains are easy to forget until one lapses at the worst possible moment. OneGlue now tracks your domains and their DNS the same way it tracks everything else — with live data pulled straight from the source, so what you see is always current rather than a snapshot someone typed in months ago.

## Highlights

- **Live DNS records from your registrar.** Pull real DNS records and domain details directly via supported registrars, so you're looking at the truth instead of hand-maintained notes that quietly go stale.
- **Expiry tracking and reminders.** OneGlue tracks expiry dates and can notify the right people ahead of time, so a renewal never slips through the cracks and takes a service down with it.
- **Domain health at a glance.** See DNS and WHOIS health for all your domains in one view, making it easy to spot a misconfiguration or an upcoming problem before it bites.
