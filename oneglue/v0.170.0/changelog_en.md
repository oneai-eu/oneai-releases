# OneGlue v0.170.0 — What's New

**Release Date:** 2026-07-07

Managing lots of devices and organizations just got quicker.

## Faster device & organization pickers

Anywhere you pick an organization or a device — enrolling a device agent, moving an agent to another organization, or linking an agent to its device — the dropdown is now a **search box**: start typing and the matching entries appear instantly. No more scrolling through hundreds of items. A real time-saver in larger environments.

## Smarter device-agent enrollment

When you roll out a OneGlue device agent, it now links itself to the correct device automatically when *both* the hostname and the IP address match — no manual step. When the match isn't certain, you get a one-click **"Confirm detected match"** instead of hunting through a list.
