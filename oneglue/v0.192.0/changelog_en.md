# OneGlue v0.192.0 — What's New

**Release Date:** 2026-07-10

Your organization's VM fleet, now visible.

## See your organization's virtual machines

Members of an organization can now view the SCVMM virtual machines that belong to their organization — directly under the organization, read-only. A new VM Fleet page lists the org's private clouds and the VMs in them (name, status, operating system, CPU/RAM, host, IP addresses), grouped by cloud and searchable. The VM Fleet entry appears in an organization's menu only when SCVMM clouds are mapped to it, so it stays out of the way for organizations that don't use it.

Each organization sees strictly its own mapped clouds' VMs — nothing from other organizations.
