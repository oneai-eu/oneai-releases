# OneGlue v0.83.0 — Backups you can trust — and trigger yourself

**Release Date:** 2026-06-03

Backups only matter if you're confident they'll be there — and that they'll actually restore — when you need them. OneGlue backs up your data automatically, and this milestone puts that safety net in your own hands, with visibility and control instead of blind faith in a scheduled job.

## Highlights

- **Trigger a backup on demand.** Kick off a backup right from the app and watch its status, so before a risky change or a big import you can capture a fresh, known-good copy without waiting for the next scheduled run.
- **Confidence in restores.** We added restore safeguards and health checks, so a backup isn't just taken and forgotten — it's verified to be genuinely recoverable, which is the part that counts on the day you actually need it.
- **Encrypted and protected.** Backups are encrypted and protected against tampering and accidental loss, keeping your most sensitive records safe even in a worst-case scenario.
